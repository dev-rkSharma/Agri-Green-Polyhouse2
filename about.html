Our Journey in Agricultural Innovation

Since 2010, Keisha Greens has been at the forefront of controlled environment agriculture, transforming how crops are grown through sustainable and efficient technologies.

Pioneering Sustainable Agriculture Since 2010

Keisha Greens was founded with a clear mission: to revolutionize conventional farming by introducing modern, sustainable, and resource-efficient agricultural technologies adapted for Indian conditions and beyond.

What began as a small initiative in Gujarat has now expanded into a global enterprise, helping farmers increase their yields while significantly reducing water consumption and eliminating the need for harmful pesticides.

Today, we offer comprehensive solutions across the agricultural spectrum, from advanced greenhouse technologies and hydroponics systems to IoT automation and marketing support – a true one-stop solution for next-generation farming.
